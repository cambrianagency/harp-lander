What We Do

Organic Instagram Advertising

How does this work? We start by searching instagram by follower counts, hashtags, geographical locations, and photo types. We make sure the results are high quality, and we connect them with local businesses that are signed up with us. Local businesses then make an offer—for example, "Free Coffee and Pastry for one Post". The instagrammer will accept the offer, post a photo in your shop for a free coffee and pastry. This is subtle but effective advertising for local businesses. Sign up for email updates above and join our network for free!
